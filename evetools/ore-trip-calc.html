Eve Ore trip optimisation calculator

This is a tool to tell where given the number of minereals you need, it will tell you what ore to mine and how many trips it will take to get them based on your skills. Currently it is not set up to tell you trip number but is will tell you optimal ore to mine. Enter your reprocessing skills as if they were percentages. For example if your common ore reprocess is 30% enter 30.
